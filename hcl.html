WELCOME TO HCL

HCL Technologies has a strong presence in Bangalore, one of India’s biggest IT hubs. The company operates multiple campuses here, including large facilities in Jigani and Manyata Tech Park, Nagawara. These centers handle diverse services such as IT consulting, engineering R\&D, digital transformation, software development, and product support. With world-class infrastructure, green campuses, and employee-friendly amenities, HCL’s Bangalore offices play a key role in serving global clients across industries like banking, healthcare, telecom, and manufacturing.

The work culture in HCL Bangalore emphasizes learning, innovation, and collaboration, supported by a wide talent pool in the city. Employees highlight opportunities to upskill and grow, while the company’s focus on digital solutions, cloud, and AI projects ensures exposure to cutting-edge technologies. As one of the major contributors to HCL’s global operations, the Bangalore centers reflect the city’s status as India’s “Silicon Valley” and continue to attract both freshers and experienced professionals seeking challenging careers in IT.
