WELCOME TO IBM

IBM has a strong presence in Bangalore, which serves as one of its biggest hubs in India and the Asia-Pacific region. The company operates multiple offices, including large campuses at Embassy GolfLinks and DivyaSree Greens, housing thousands of employees. These centers focus on delivering global IT services, consulting, cloud, and AI solutions. IBM has also established a Security Command Center and SOC in Bangalore, playing a critical role in monitoring and responding to cyber threats worldwide. In addition, its Innovation Centre partners with startups and academia in areas like IoT, AI, and big data, strengthening Bangalore’s reputation as a global technologyhub.

Bangalore is strategically important to IBM because of its vast talent pool, thriving startup ecosystem, and cost-effective infrastructure. The city enables IBM to manage key client operations, build advanced digital solutions, and drive innovation for industries like aviation, banking, and telecom. With partnerships such as the one with Bangalore International Airport Limited (BIAL) for digital transformation, IBM continues to expand its role in shaping India’s and the region’s technology landscape, making Bangalore a cornerstone of its global operations.
